Falcon 9/Dragon CRS-19 Mission

As of November 20, the Falcon 9/Dragon CRS-19 launch to the International Space Station is scheduled for No Earlier Than Wednesday, December 4 at 12:48 PM EDT / 17:48 UTC from launch pad SLC-40 at Cape Canaveral Air Force Station. This launch will feature a flight-proven Falcon 9 booster lofting a Dragon cargo ship to the ISS, transporting experiments and supplies to the station as well as research payloads, and will also include a spectacular Return To Launch Site booster landing.

STATUS UPDATE: We are currently GO for a Star✦Fleet mission to view this launch! The boats are go, cookies are go, on-site volunteers are go and the mission patch, stickers and T-shirts are in work. Our reservation system go live at *17:00 UTC (12:00 noon EST) this Saturday, November 23rd, so be ready to order as tickets tend to go fast! In the meantime, make sure to stay tuned to our Slack, email list and social media for the latest important updates and to get in touch with us directly (see below for links). Thanks!

Rocket Launch Viewing

Price: $75 per person (including OO2 upper deck if available)
Key Times: Checkin 10:45AM | Boarding 11:15AM | Departure 11:45AM

  • Unbeatable location directly between the three landings (see map)
  • Unobstructed view of SLC-40, LZ-1, and many other historic CCAFS pads
  • Choice of OO2 lower deck, OO2 upper deck, TNT1 and TNT2, and ocean or river
  • Pre-mission genuine NASA launch cookie bake sale to support SCLA
  • Free water, drinks and anti-nausea tablets included with ticket
  • Includes a custom decal of the Star✦Fleet mission patch
  • First 20 people get a coveted spot on OO2 obs deck, subject to availability

Guided Pre-Launch Pad Tours

Price: $70 per person (with launch ticket); $80 (without)
Launch day tour time: Typically from T-6 to T-3 hours, if offered

  • Get an exclusive, up-close look at the rocket on the pad typically unavailable from any other angle
  • See numerous historic Explorer, Mercury, Gemini, Apollo and Shuttle launch sites
  • Enjoy a unique ocean-side view of every past, present and future CCAFS and KSC pad
  • View many sites of history interest in Port Canaveral harbor and along the Cape
  • Smaller boat can get right up to the beach to provide an optimal view
  • Tour led by Julia Bergeron, local history expert and space personality, and founder of the Space Coast Launch Ambassadors
  • Numerous opportunities to stop and take photos
  • $10 discount per ticket for purchasing launch ticket
  • Includes a custom decal of the Star✦Fleet mission patch (if offered)
  • Comes with a genuine home-made NASA "launch day cookie" and a card with the original NASA recipe (see below)

ESA Solar Orbiter Launch

As of February 4, the launch of the Solar Orbiter mission for ESA on an Atlas V is scheduled for No Earlier Than Sunday, February 9 at 11:03 PM EST / Monday, February 10 at 04:03 UTC from launch pad SLC-41 at the Cape Canaveral Air Force Station in Florida.

For this mission, we will be hosting exclusive pre-launch closeup boat tours of the pad, rocket and rollout to help accommodate, among others, NASA's special invited media and guests from the NASA Social. We'll also consider a boat trip to view the launch itself if sufficient interest develops. Let us know if you're interested in the latter, and stay tuned for more information on that. Based on the hazard zones, we would offer a uniquely up-close view of the trajectory, at a competitive price to KSCVC options.
